

The Smart Arduino Blind Stick (SABS) represents a significant advancement in assistive technology for visually impaired individuals. Developed with a focus on affordability, functionality, and user-friendliness, the SABS integrates multiple features into a single device, including obstacle detection, object identification, water hazard sensing, GPS navigation, camera-based object detection, voice output functionality, and an emergency button. The SABS boasts several blessings and represents numerous technical breakthroughs, including significant cost advantages. By providing an affordable, versatile, and user-centric assistive tool, the SABS aims to improve the quality of life for visually impaired individuals and promote inclusivity in society. Through a systematic approach encompassing hardware setup, software development, integration, testing, maintenance, user support, monitoring, and documentation, the SABS ensures reliability, effectiveness, and accessibility.
